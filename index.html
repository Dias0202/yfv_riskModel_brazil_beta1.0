Yellow Fever Risk Modelling – Brazil

Yellow fever predicted risk across Brazilian municipalities

This site presents a project where historical outbreaks, vaccination coverage, land-use change, population dynamics and climate projections are integrated into a machine-learning model to estimate the future risk of yellow fever (YF) occurrence in Brazil (2021–2040).
